About Bean&Brew

Our Story

At Bean&Brew, we believe that a great cup of coffee is more than just a morning ritual—it's a moment of connection. Founded by a group of lifelong friends and caffeine enthusiasts, our shop was born out of a simple desire to create a "third place" where the community could gather, recharge, and find inspiration. We pride ourselves on our meticulous approach to sourcing, working directly with small-scale farmers to ensure every bean is grown sustainably and roasted to its peak potential. From the first grind to the final pour, our baristas treat coffee as a craft, dedicated to bringing out the unique notes and stories behind every origin.

Why Bean&Brew?

🍵

Ethically Sourced

We prioritize direct-trade relationships to ensure farmers are paid fairly and use eco-friendly practices.

🧉

Small-Batch Roasting

To guarantee freshness, we roast our beans in small quantities, preserving the delicate oils and aromas.

👪

Community First

We aren't just a business; we’re your neighbors. We host local art pop-ups and open mic nights to keep the local spirit alive.
